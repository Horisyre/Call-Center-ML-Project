## 7. Operational Insights

- Operational Expenditure (OPEX) for the call center is driven by several factors, including the following:
  - Phone lines provisioned → higher lines mean higher rental, maintenance, and telephony costs.
  - Channels provisioned → each channel (chat, email, voice, social) may require software, licenses or agent time.
  - Volume of interactions → more interactions might require more staff hours or automation, it may also affect channels provisioned indirectly affecting OPEX.

From our correlation matrix, we observe relatively small to moderate relationships between interactions, phone calls and channels provisioned. However, we know these factors are interdependent. The relatively low correlations may indicate that, because fluctuations in lines provisioned and other resources are small, the system is generally able to meet demand without major strain.
