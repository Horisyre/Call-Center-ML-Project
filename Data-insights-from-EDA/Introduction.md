# Introduction

- This dataset contains monthly call center operational data across multiple industries.
- Key variables include call volumes, number of phone lines and number of communication channels used.
- The purpose of this analysis is to explore the dataset to identify patterns, trends and potential anomalies.
- Insights from this analysis may support forecasting, resource allocation and operational planning within call center environments.

---

## Data Summary

- The dataset records monthly call center activity over a multi-year period, allowing for the analysis of long-term operational trends.

- Each observation represents monthly aggregated call center activity for a specific industry.

- Variables in the dataset include:

  - Monthly interactions volume

  - Number of phone lines

  - Number of communication channels (total active channels available for customer interactions, possibly including phone lines, email, chat and other digital platforms.)

  - Industry classification (e.g., Telecoms, Healthcare)

- Further analysis, including trend analysis, correlation analysis, and anomaly detection, is presented in separate sections of this report.
